Missouri's Vaccine Navigator

The Missouri Vaccine Navigator is intended to help individuals navigate the COVID-19 vaccination process. This tool may not capture every event in your area, so we encourage you to engage with your local public health officials and health care facilities to learn more.

Important Note:

We encourage individuals to register online if they have the ability. To ensure that the vaccine is available to all Missourians, regardless of internet and computer access, we have staff available to help individuals register through our COVID-19 hotline at (877) 435-8411.(877) 435-8411.

Step 1

Registration

Sign up to receive a patient ID number, which will help you enter the scheduling feature of the Navigator.

Step 2

Schedule

Identify events in your area, and schedule an appointment.


Step 3

Begin Vaccination

Receive Dose 1 of the COVID-19
vaccine.

Step 4

Get Second Dose

Return to the same provider for Dose 2 of the vaccine (Moderna or Pfizer only).

Resident FAQs

What happens if I do not have an email?

If you do not have an email, please provide a cell phone or landline so our COVID-19 Hotline team can reach out to you to schedule your appointment for you when you are eligible.
  • If you provided a cell phone number for a smart phone, then you will still receive all the scheduled appointment and vaccination received confirmations
  • If you provided only a landline or cell phone number for a non-smart phone, then you will need to contact the COVID-19 Hotline at (877) 435-8411 to have your scheduled appointment or vaccination confirmation information provided verbally over the phone

What do I do if I do not see any available appointments?

• If there are no appointments on your scheduling page, select “None of these appointments work for me” to receive a reminder to schedule at a later date. Additionally, new appointments are added every day so please check back periodically using the scheduling link provided in your invite email or click here.

Step 1:

Complete registration to receive a patient ID number.
  • Complete the online form to register for a COVID-19 vaccine
  • You will be asked to provide the following:
    • Information about you and your medical history
    • Contact information you check often, so you can be reached to make an appointment
  • All information is confidential and will only be shared as appropriate
  • You will receive an email confirmation once your registration is successfully completed
  • Click here to view our frequently asked questions
  • Click here to register

Step 2:

Schedule an appointment
  • You will get a text or email from our system to schedule an appointment. You can also schedule an appointment using your patient ID number by clicking here.
  • Once you make your appointment, you will be asked to print a confirmation to bring to your appointment
    • If you do not have access to a printer, you may also show your confirmation using a device with internet access

Step 3:

Receive dose 1 of the COVID-19 vaccine
  • Bring your confirmation information to your appointment by printing or ensuring easy access from your device
  • At your scheduled time and date, go to the designated location and follow instructions
  • Please wear your face covering and remember to stay 6 feet apart

Step 4:

Receive dose 2 of the COVID-19 vaccine
  • You need two doses of the Pfizer and Moderna vaccines to protect you from the virus. A second dose of Pfizer can be scheduled at least 21 days after the first dose, and a second dose of Moderna can be scheduled at least 28 days after the first dose. Johnson & Johnson’s Janssen vaccine is one dose only.
  • You will receive a text or email with instructions on returning for your second dose
  • Continue to practice the 3 Ws:
    • Wash your hands
    • Wear your face covering
    • Watch your distance

There are three ways to get the vaccine. It’s your choice.
Have more questions? Call us at 877-435-8411.
