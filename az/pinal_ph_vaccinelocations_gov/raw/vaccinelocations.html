How to Register for COVID-19 Vaccines at Pinal County Public Health Clinics:

You must schedule an appointment in advance. Please call the Central Appointment Desk at
1-866-960-0633 to schedule an appointment.


  • Wear a face mask to your appointment.
  • You may arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment time.
  • If you have health insurance, bring your insurance card and photo ID.
  • If you have a fever, are moderately ill or in quarantine, please reschedule your appointment.


    Please fill out the forms below, print out the filled form, AND bring it to your scheduled Public Health vaccination appointment. Please do not mail forms into our Public Health Admin building. The link includes the Consent Form and Patient's Rights & Responsibilities Form.

  • Click here to fill and print Consent Form and Patients & Responsibilities Form
  • Click here to fill and print forms in Spanish.


NOTE: FOR PINAL COUNTY PUBLIC HEALTH CLINICS

Please be aware that you MUST have an appointment to be vaccinated at our Public Health Clinics, walk-ins are not accepted.

Staff will contact the authorities in the event they receive threats or verbal abuse. We thank you for your patience as we work with the current limitations around vaccine supply to the County.
