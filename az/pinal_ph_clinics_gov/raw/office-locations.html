Latest Updates from Public Health Clinics

Effective 2/1/21: we have temporarily made some changes to our hours of operations for Nursing Services ONLY. For Nursing Clinic hours and appointments please contact our Central Appointment Desk. We apologize for any inconvenience.

At this time, we are requiring all individuals coming in for services in any of our Pinal County Public Health Clinics to please be prepared and arrive with a mask on. This will not only protect yourself, but also others around you.

Clinic Locations and Scheduling an Appointment:

Note: Clinic Closure Dates

For general information, or to schedule appointments for Woman, Infants and Children (WIC), Well Woman HealthCheck, Family Planning , or STD Treatment, please call the Central Appointment Desk toll free at:

1-866-960-0633 between Monday through Friday from 8:00am to 5:30pm

Please note due to the pandemic, the clinics hours may vary. We encourage you to call 1-866-960-0633 to verify the most up to date hours. All required and recommended Childhood Immunizations are provided at no cost to you for kids under the age of 18 and is available by appointment only. At this time, immunizations are only available at our Apache Junction, Casa Grande, San Tan Valley, and Mammoth clinics.

Please bring records your complete immunization records to your appointment.  
 
Pinal County Public Health accepts most insurance plans. Most nursing services are available on a sliding fee scale based on family income and number of household members. No one is refused service due to inability to pay. Payments and donations are accepted in cash, credit or debit. Please visit our Online Payment webpage for more information.
