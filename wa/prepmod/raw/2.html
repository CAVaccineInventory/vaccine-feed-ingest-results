Vaccinations offered: Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 Vaccine, Janssen COVID-19 Vaccine

Age groups served: Adults, Seniors, Other

Services offered: COVID-19 Vaccination

Additional Information: The Ridgefield High School vaccination site is providing the Johnson & Johnson vaccine and the Pfizer vaccine. The Johnson & Johnson vaccine requires only one dose; you will not need to return for a second dose. The Pfizer vaccine requires two doses; if this is your first dose, you will need to return for a second dose. Please do not arrive more than 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment. Arriving too early can lead to longer waiting times. You will be asked to remain at the vaccination site for 15 to 30 minutes after being vaccinated. Medical personnel will be monitoring you for possible reactions. Severe reactions are rare. What to bring: • Identification: If you have identification or some other proof of your date of birth, please bring it. This helps to ensure registration information is accurate. But ID is not required for vaccination. • Face covering: Face coverings must be worn while at the COVID-19 vaccination site. • Please wear a short-sleeved shirt or shirt with loose sleeves that can be pulled up. La vacuna Pfizer requiere dos dosis; si esta es su primera dosis, tendrá que volver para una segunda dosis. Por favor, no llegue más de 10 minutos antes de su cita. Llegar con demasiada antelación puede causar tiempos de espera más largos. Se le pedirá que permanezca en el centro de vacunación de 15 a 30 minutos después de ser vacunado. El personal médico le vigilará para detectar posibles reacciones. Las reacciones graves son poco frecuentes. Qué debe traer: • Identificación: Si tiene un documento de identitificación, o documento con fecha de nacimiento, por favor tráigalo. Esto ayuda a asegurar que la información de registro es correcta.
