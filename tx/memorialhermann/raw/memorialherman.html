COVID-19 Vaccine Walk-In Clinics

Memorial Hermann is pleased to offer free COVID-19 vaccination to community members across Greater Houston. To provide additional, convenient access to the vaccine, first- and second-dose walk-ins are now being accepted at select Memorial Hermann hospitals and clinics for individuals 12 years of age and older. Please note: Minors (individuals 12 through 17 years of age) will be required to have an adult accompany them to their vaccination appointment. In addition, should individuals need assistance receiving vaccination, one caregiver may accompany them to their appointment.

Memorial Hermann locations administering the Moderna vaccine will only vaccinate individuals 18 years of age and older.

To view walk-in locations and hours, please reference the list below. This list is subject to change based on vaccine demand. For questions, we encourage you to call Memorial Hermann’s COVID-19 Vaccine Helpline at (833) 772-2864.
