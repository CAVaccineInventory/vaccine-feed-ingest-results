How to Register for COVID-19 Vaccines at County Locations:

You must schedule an appointment in advance for County locations. State locations do accept walk-ins.
Please call or go to the link to register at the site you prefer.

Please follow the information & instructions for each location:

  • Wear a face mask to your appointment.
  • Arrive as close to your scheduled appointment as possible.
  • If you have health insurance, bring your insurance card and photo ID.
  • If you have a fever, are moderately ill or in quarantine, please reschedule your appointment.

For our Public Health Clinic Locations:
  • If anyone does not have internet access and needs help signing up, please call the Central Appointment Desk for help at 1-866-960-0633.

    • Please fill out the forms below, print out the filled form, AND bring it to your scheduled Public Health vaccination appointment. Please do not mail forms into our Public Health Admin building. The link includes the Consent Form and Patient's Rights & Responsibilities Form.

  • Click here to fill and print Consent Form and Patients & Responsibilities Form
  • Click here to fill and print forms in Spanish.

Please understand due to limited vaccine supply, appointments may be several weeks out. Appointments are only available when vaccine is available.


Use the search input and type your city to find the nearest site to you.
Please check the instructions for each location.
